# Copywriting Operating Rules

## Default delivery standard
- All copy deliverables must be formal, properly punctuated, and internal-review ready.

## Readability standard
- Default target is approximately Grade 6 readability.
- Write with short sentences, simple words, and clean structure.
- Avoid jargon unless necessary for technical accuracy.
- Prioritise clarity over cleverness.

## Application scope
- Headlines
- Ad copy
- Captions
- Scripts
- Funnel sections
- Emails

## Toward vs Away Language (Alen Sultanic)

Two emotional registers to deploy strategically:

- **Away language** (escape the pain): drives cold traffic action but attracts fear-based buyers. Higher refunds, weaker LTV, shallower brand relationship.
- **Toward language** (achieve the goal, become the person): attracts identity-aligned buyers with stronger conviction. Better LTV, lower refund rates, deeper brand loyalty.

**The play:** use away hooks to interrupt and qualify at the top of funnel. Once they're engaged, shift to toward language. The buyer you attract with desire is worth more than the one you attract with fear.

**Practical application:**
- Cold traffic hooks: lean away ("Stop wasting money on ads that don't convert")
- Body copy and landing page: shift toward ("Build a lead machine that runs without you")
- Offer framing: always close on toward (identity, aspiration, transformation)

## Override rule
- If Jasper explicitly requests a different tone or reading level for a specific asset, follow that request.
