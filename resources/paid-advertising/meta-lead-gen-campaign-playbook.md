# Meta Lead Gen Campaign Playbook

Universal playbook for client acquisition and lead generation campaigns on Meta.

Last updated: 2026-03-16

---

## The System

Two campaigns running simultaneously. **Test** finds winners at low spend. **Scale** puts real budget behind proven winners. Winners move from test to scale. Test gets replenished with fresh angles. Repeat forever.

---

## Campaign 1: Test Campaign

**Type:** ABO
**Structure:** 20 ad sets, 1 creative per ad set
**Budget:** $3-5/day per ad set

- Do not touch anything for 72 hours.

---

## Step 1: The 72-Hour Analysis

**If leads have come in**, cut any ad set that meets any of the following:

- CPL above your target
- Landing page conversion rate below 10%
- Hasn't spent enough to give reliable data

**If no leads yet**, cut any ad set with CTR below 1.25%. Keep everything above it.

What remains are your winners.

---

## Step 2: Move Winners to Scale

Take your winners and create variants of each. Change one element per variant: hook, visual, or format.

Move all of them into the scale campaign as a new ad set.

---

## Campaign 2: Scale Campaign

**Type:** ABO
**Structure:** Multiple ad sets, each one is a pack of winners and their variants
**Budget:** $50-100/day per ad set

**Rules:**

- Each new batch of winners becomes its own ad set.
- Never touch an ad set that is hitting your CPL target. Leave it completely alone.
- New winners always go into a brand new ad set, never added into an existing one.

**Scaling the budget:**

- Increase each ad set budget by 15% every 2 days.
- Only increase if CPL is on target.

---

## Step 3: Replenish the Test Campaign

After every analysis, bring the test campaign back to 20 active ad sets with:

- Variations of winning angles
- Fresh concepts that haven't been tested yet

---

## Weekly Rhythm

**Monday:** Replenish test campaign to 20 active ad sets.

**Thursday:** 72-hour mark. Run the analysis. Cut losers. Move winners to scale. Note winning angles for next week's variations.

**Daily:** Check scale campaign CPL. If it's on target, don't touch it.

---

## The Loop

```
20 creatives in test (ABO, $3-5/ad set)
               ↓
     72 hours, hands off
               ↓
  No leads: cut below 1.25% CTR
  Leads: cut above CPL target + LP below 10%
               ↓
   Take winners, create variants of each
               ↓
  New ad set in scale campaign (ABO, $50-100/day)
               ↓
    Scale 15% every 2 days if CPL on target
               ↓
      Never touch ad sets that are working
               ↓
  Replenish test with variations + fresh angles
               ↓
          Repeat every week
```
