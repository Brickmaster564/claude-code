# 5. Objection-Handling Copy Blocks

## "It's a scam."

We understand your skepticism. The tax relief industry has some bad
actors. That's why we are A+ Rated by the Better Business Bureau and are
staffed by licensed Tax Attorneys and Enrolled Agents.

## "I can do it myself."

While you have the right to represent yourself, do you know the specific
forms, deadlines, and negotiation tactics to get the best possible
outcome?

## "It's too expensive."

What is the cost of not getting help? Continued penalties, interest, and
the threat of wage garnishment or bank levy can cost far more.

## "The IRS won't negotiate."

The IRS's primary goal is to collect what it is owed. They often prefer
a guaranteed smaller settlement over chasing a debt they may never fully
collect.
