# Business Loans Lead Buyers -- U.S. Market Research

**Compiled:** 2026-03-31

---

## Industry Context

The U.S. alternative business lending market is projected to exceed $62 billion in 2025, with the broader online lending platform segment growing at a 25% CAGR toward $14.5 billion by 2030. The SBA 7(a) program alone approved approximately 77,600 loans totaling $37 billion in FY2025, a 44.7% increase over FY2024. The U.S. merchant cash advance market reached an estimated $20 billion in annual origination volume and is projected to grow to $26 billion or more by 2026.

**Lead Pricing Benchmarks (2026):**
- Exclusive fresh business loan leads: $25 to $100 per lead (varies by loan product and qualification level)
- Exclusive MCA leads: $50 to $120 per lead
- Live transfer leads: $75 to $200 per transfer
- Aged leads (30-180+ days): $0.50 to $5.00 per record
- UCC leads: $1 to $5 per record
- Financial services CPL via paid ads (Google/Meta): $80 to $650+, depending on channel and targeting

**Customer LTV Context:** Business lenders typically target an LTV-to-CAC ratio of 3:1 or higher. A single funded business loan can generate $5,000 to $50,000+ in revenue depending on product type, making lead acquisition at $25 to $200 per lead highly profitable when conversion rates hold at 5-15%.

## TIER 2 -- Merchant Cash Advance Companies

MCA companies often have the loosest qualification requirements, the fastest funding times, and some of the highest margins, which translates into aggressive lead purchasing behavior. The U.S. MCA market is estimated at $20B+ in annual originations and growing at approximately 7% CAGR.
