## Key Network Metrics to Track

When testing through networks, measure these to build your case for direct dealer negotiations:

| Metric | What to Track | Target |
|---|---|---|
| **Acceptance rate** | % of leads the network accepts as valid | >85% |
| **Chargeback rate** | % of leads returned/credited | <15% |
| **CPL paid to you** | What the network pays per accepted lead | $25-$50 (networks take margin) |
| **Speed to acceptance** | How fast they confirm/reject leads | <24 hours |
| **Volume capacity** | How many leads/week they can absorb | Depends on geo |

**Important:** Networks pay you LESS than direct dealers because they take a margin. Expect $25-$50 from networks vs $60-$120 from direct dealerships. The network phase is for VALIDATION, not long-term revenue.

## Chargeback Policies (Industry Standard)

Most networks offer credits for invalid leads. Typical eligible reasons:
- Disconnected/wrong phone number
- Person denies requesting service
- Renter (not homeowner/decision-maker)
- Outside service area
- Duplicate lead

**Typical dispute window:** 3-7 days from lead delivery.

Confirmed policies:
- **Billy.com:** Lead credits for invalid contact info
- **33 Mile Radius:** No billing for leads that fail validation
- **DOPPCALL:** Lead not billed if criteria not met
- **Angi:** Credit for fake numbers, renters, wrong area

## Testing Playbook

1. **Week 1-2:** Sign up with Billy.com (pre-paid, no risk). Send 20-30 leads. Measure acceptance rate and chargeback rate.
2. **Week 3-4:** Add DOPPCALL or 33 Mile Radius for exclusive/call testing. Send 20-30 leads. Compare conversion data.
3. **Week 5+:** Use your acceptance rate, chargeback rate, and CPL data to pitch direct to dealerships (Safe Haven, SafeStreets, Alert 360). Your pitch: "I've validated X% acceptance rate through network testing. I'm offering exclusive leads at $[X] with a 5-day chargeback window."
4. **Ongoing:** Route surplus and out-of-territory leads to networks. Direct dealer relationships become your primary revenue.
