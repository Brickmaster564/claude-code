# Top 50 Home Security Dealership Targets

**Purpose:** Priority outreach list for selling exclusive homeowner leads direct to dealerships.
**Strategy:** Work top-down. Tier 1 first (largest appetite), then expand to regional players.
**Prepared:** March 2026

> Manufacturers (ADT, Vivint, Brinks) are NOT on this list. They don't buy leads directly. Their authorized dealerships do.

## Outreach Priority Summary

**Start with these 5 first:**
1. Safe Haven Security (largest independent ADT dealer, 43 states)
2. SafeStreets USA (ADT's oldest dealer, 39 states, $80-$120 CPL)
3. Skyline Security (largest Brinks dealer, 36 states, 43K installs/year)
4. Alert 360 (own brand, nationwide, actively seeking lead partners)
5. Secure24 (largest ADT dealer by coverage, 47 states)

**Then expand to regional players matching your ad geo:**
- Florida: SafeTouch, Crime Prevention Security
- Texas: Smith Thompson, DFW Security, Devcon
- California: Bay Alarm, SSD Alarm
- Georgia/Southeast: Ackerman, EMC, CPI
- Northeast: Slomins, Beacon, Doyle, Alarm New England
- Midwest: Guardian Alarm (MI), Per Mar, EPS, Habitec
