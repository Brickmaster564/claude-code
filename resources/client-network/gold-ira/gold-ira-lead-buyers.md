## Market Overview

The precious metals IRA industry is projected to reach $22.6 billion by 2027, representing nearly 180% growth from 2020. Gold has risen roughly 60% since early 2025, driving record demand. The average gold IRA investment jumped from $35,000 in 2023 to $100,000 in 2024, making each lead significantly more valuable.

In 2014, fewer than 10 companies actively marketed gold IRA investments. By 2024, that number surged to over 100. Americans collectively hold around $14.3 trillion in IRA accounts, and gold IRAs represent a fast-growing slice of that pie.

For a PPL (pay-per-lead) business, this market is ideal:
- **High ticket values** ($10K-$100K+ per customer) mean companies can afford $50-$200+ per qualified lead
- **Long sales cycles** with consultative selling make warm, pre-qualified leads essential
- **Heavy competition** forces companies to diversify lead sources beyond their own advertising
- **Affiliate programs already exist** at most major companies, proving the lead-buying model works
- **Conservative/retirement demographic** responds well to educational content funnels

## Tier 4: Financial Advisors & Wealth Managers

Independent financial advisors who specialize in alternative investments represent a large but fragmented market for gold IRA leads. They don't buy leads at the same volume as the dealers, but there are thousands of them.

### Key Segments

**Self-Directed IRA Custodians (referral partners, not direct lead buyers):**
- **GoldStar Trust Company** (Amarillo, TX): $2.5B in assets, 37,000+ self-directed IRAs. A trust-only branch of Happy State Bank. They don't buy leads directly but work with precious metals dealers who do.
- **Equity Trust Company**: Major self-directed IRA custodian with MetalsConnect platform. Partners with hundreds of precious metals dealers.
- **STRATA Trust Company**: Provides custody for alternative and traditional assets. Works with gold IRA companies as a backend custodian.
- **IRA Financial** (New York): Specializes in self-directed retirement accounts. Low fees, flexible investment options.

**Independent Financial Advisors:**
- Fee-only advisors who recommend alternative investments (5-15% precious metals allocation)
- Registered Investment Advisors (RIAs) with retired/near-retirement client bases
- Insurance agents who cross-sell retirement products
- Tax professionals who advise on IRA rollovers

**How to reach them:**
- Financial advisor conferences (see Events section below)
- LinkedIn prospecting targeting "alternative investments" + "financial advisor"
- Referral programs through the custodians listed above
- Content marketing targeting "how to recommend gold IRAs to clients"

**Lead Buying Likelihood: Low-Medium per individual, but significant in aggregate.** A single advisor might buy 5-10 leads per month, but there are tens of thousands of advisors.

### CPA Networks with Gold IRA Offers

Gold IRA offers are among the highest-paying CPA verticals in financial services. Key characteristics:
- Payouts range from $25 (Birch Gold, per qualified lead) to $500 (Augusta, per qualified lead)
- Revenue share models add 2-13% of account value on conversion
- Average gold IRA investment of $100K means a single conversion can generate $2,000-$13,000 in affiliate revenue
- Most programs require phone-verified leads with minimum investment intent ($10K-$50K)
