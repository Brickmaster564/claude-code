# Gold IRA Offer Sheet

**Last updated:** 2026-03-23

## Tier 1: Comparison Table Clickouts

**Model:** Outbound traffic via comparison content. Prospects click through to partner landing pages or call lines. No exclusive data capture on our end.

**Lead type:** Shared/non-exclusive clickout
**Sell price:** $40 - $50 per lead
**Qualification:** Self-selected interest (clicked through from comparison content). Not phone-verified, not asset-qualified.

**Use cases:**
- Affiliate comparison sites
- Content-driven SEO pages ("Best Gold IRA Companies 2026")
- Native ad advertorials with embedded partner links

**Buyer profile:** Gold IRA dealers running broad top-of-funnel acquisition, affiliate networks, lead aggregators.

## Tier 2: Exclusive Data Leads (Quiz Funnel)

**Model:** Paid media (Meta, Google) driving traffic into a quiz funnel. Prospects self-qualify through quiz steps, submit contact details, and are routed exclusively to a single partner.

**Lead type:** Exclusive, real-time delivery
**CPQL (Cost Per Qualified Lead):** $200 - $300
**RevShare:** 7 - 10% on funded account trade value
**Qualification criteria (Augusta standard):**
- Valid US phone number
- Valid email address
- Has eligible IRA or 401(k) with $50,000+ that can be rolled over
- Not already in the partner's database
- Dispositioned as qualified by the partner's sales team

**Current partner:** Augusta Precious Metals
**Deal structure (Augusta, Prime tier):**
- $200 per Qualified Lead
- 8% commission on Premium Trade Value
- 0% on bullion
- VIP upgrade (at Augusta's discretion): $200/QL + 10% premium + 1% bullion

**Funnel flow:**
1. Ad (Meta/Google) > Quiz landing page
2. Step 1: "Which best describes your situation?" (intent segmentation)
3. Step 2: "How much are you looking to move into gold?" (asset qualification, $50K minimum)
4. Step 3: "What type of account do you have?" (401k, IRA, TSP)
5. Step 4: Lead capture (name, email, mobile)
6. Under $50K path: soft rejection + email capture for nurture
7. Qualified path: thank you page with Augusta branding, prospect expects a call

**Revenue math (per 100 raw leads at $50 CPL):**

| Metric | Value |
|---|---|
| Ad spend | $5,000 |
| Raw leads | 100 |
| Qualification rate | 20% |
| Qualified leads | 20 |
| Upfront revenue ($200 x 20) | $4,000 |

| Avg rollover | $100,000 |
| Commission per funded (8%) | $8,000 |
| Total commission revenue | $24,000 |
| **Total revenue** | **$28,000** |
| **Profit** | **$23,000** |

## Notes

- Tier 1 is lower margin but lower risk, no ad spend required if running organic/SEO content.
- Tier 2 is where the real money is. The $200/lead covers ad spend, and the rev share on funded accounts is pure profit.
- Augusta's typical order sizes: $250K / $500K / $1M. A single funded account at 8% on a $250K trade = $20,000 commission.
- Augusta compliance is strict. All copy must be approved before publishing. No fearmongering, no financial advice, no "diversify" language. See affiliate agreement and compliance manual for full details.
