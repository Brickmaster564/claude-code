# Senior Care Headlines Swipe File

## Headlines by Angle & Awareness Stage

### Unaware — Curiosity / Pattern Interrupt
- Your Parent Smiled and Said "I'm Fine." Here's What That Usually Means After 65.
- 11,400 Americans Turned 65 Today. Is Your Family Ready For What Comes Next?

### Fear / Safety — Problem Aware
- Every 11 Seconds, a Senior Visits the ER After a Fall. Is Your Parent Home Alone Right Now?
- 70% of People Over 65 Will Need Long-Term Care. Most Families Aren't Ready.
- Your Parent Is Spending 16 Hours a Day Alone. Falls, Missed Meds, Skipped Meals. How Long Until Something Happens?
- She Fell in the Bathroom and Laid There For Hours. No One Knew.
- He's Lost 15 Pounds and You Don't Think He's Eating When You're Not There.
- Every Time Your Phone Rings, You Think It's the Hospital.
- You Found Expired Food in the Fridge and Pills She Hasn't Been Taking.
- Your Parent Got Lost Driving to the Grocery Store She's Been Going to For 30 Years.
- Mom Says She's Fine. But She's Not Showering, Not Eating Right, and Not Taking Her Medication.
- Dad Left the Stove On Again. You're 45 Minutes Away. What Happens Next Time?

### Questions / Confrontation — Problem Aware
- If Your Parent Fell Right Now, How Long Before Someone Would Find Them?
- When Was the Last Time Mom Showered? Do You Even Know?
- Is Your Parent Eating Real Meals, or Just Tea and Toast?
- How Many More "I'm Fine" Texts Before You Stop Believing Them?

### Guilt / Burnout / Identity — Problem Aware
- You Promised You'd Take Care of Her. That Doesn't Mean You Have to Do It Alone.
- Still Driving to Mom's House Every Night After Work? There's a Better Way.
- She Left the Stove On Twice Last Week. You Can't Keep Pretending Everything Is Normal.
- You're Working 50 Hours a Week and Still Checking on Mom Every Night. Something Has to Give.
- You Check the Locks, Call to Remind Her About Meds, and Still Can't Sleep. Every Single Night.
- You're the Only Sibling Who Shows Up. You're Exhausted. And Nobody's Asking How You're Doing.
- You Cry in Your Car Before Work Because You Don't Know How Much Longer You Can Do This.

### Affordability / Objection Flip — Solution Aware
- Think Senior Care is Too Expensive? Families Are Getting In-Home Help For Less Than Dining Out Once a Week.
- In-Home Care For Your Parent, Starting at $18/Hour. No Contracts. No Wait Lists.

### Simplicity / Speed — Solution Aware
- Finally, Senior Care Without the Confusion. Get Matched With a Vetted Caregiver in 48 Hours.
- Get Your Parent Setup With In-Home Care This Week (Free Care Plan)

### Aspiration / Relief — Product Aware
- What If You Could Stop Worrying Every Time Your Phone Rings? Trusted In-Home Caregivers For Your Parent.

### Geo / Localised — Solution Aware
