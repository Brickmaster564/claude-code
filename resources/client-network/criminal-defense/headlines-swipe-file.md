## Headline Testing Rules

1. **Test structure first, variables second.** Don't test "First Offense" vs. "Just Arrested" until you know if the Fear angle beats the Normalization angle.
2. **One variable change per test.** Change the audience OR the proof point, not both.
3. **Lead with the scariest number.** $25,000 stops the scroll harder than $10,000 even though both are accurate.
4. **Geo headlines are cheap wins.** Same headline, swap the state per ad set. The state name is a pattern interrupt in the feed.
5. **The 10-day deadline is the strongest urgency hook.** Use it in at least one variant per test cycle.
6. **Match terminology to state.** DWI in Texas, OVI in Ohio, OWI in Michigan. Wrong term = lost trust.
