# Personal Injury Lead Buyers -- U.S. Market Research

**Compiled: 2026-03-23**

This document catalogs companies that BUY personal injury leads in the United States, organized by tier. These are potential customers for a personal injury lead generation business.

---

## Industry Context

Personal injury law is the single highest-spending legal advertising category in the United States, and the market is enormous, highly fragmented, and hungry for leads.

**Key Statistics:**

| Metric | Value |
|---|---|
| Total PI industry revenue (U.S.) | $61.7 billion (2025, IBISWorld) |
| Number of PI attorneys | ~135,000 to 164,000 (roughly 10% of all U.S. lawyers) |
| Number of PI law firms | ~50,000 to 60,000 |
| PI claims filed annually | ~400,000 |
| Total legal services ad spend (2024) | $2.5 billion across 26.9 million ads |
| Top firm ad spend (Morgan & Morgan, 2024) | $218 million (8% of all legal ads) |
| Average MVA settlement | $20,000 to $30,000 |
| Average attorney contingency fee | 33.3% to 40% of settlement |
| Average attorney revenue per MVA case | $6,600 to $12,000 |
| Google Ads CPC for PI keywords | $100 to $500+ per click (up to $935 for mesothelioma) |
| MVA lead cost (purchased) | $300 to $1,500 per lead |
| Market concentration | No single firm holds more than 5% market share |

**Why this market is ideal for PPL lead generation:** The combination of high case values, contingency fee economics, extreme Google Ads costs, and massive fragmentation means that thousands of PI firms are actively looking for alternative lead sources. A single signed MVA case can generate $6,000 to $12,000+ in attorney fees, which means firms will pay $300 to $1,500 per qualified lead and still maintain healthy margins. The market is large enough that even capturing a tiny fraction represents significant revenue.
