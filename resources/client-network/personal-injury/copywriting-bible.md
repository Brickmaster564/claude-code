# The PPL Personal Injury / MVA Copywriting Bible

A complete swipe file for generating high-quality personal injury leads via Facebook and Google ads in the US market. Built on the Sabri Suby copywriting frameworks and heavily incorporates real consumer language from voice of customer research.

**Positioning:** The consumer-facing funnel positions the service as a "free case evaluation" or "claims review" that connects accident victims with a qualified attorney. The lead gen business (Client Network) sits behind the scenes, selling qualified leads to PI law firms.

**Readability standard:** All consumer-facing ad copy targets Grade 6. Simple words, short sentences, zero jargon unless immediately defined.

## 2. Facebook Ad Hooks

1. The insurance company's first offer is almost NEVER what your case is worth. Here's what they don't want you to know.
2. If a car accident left you buried in medical bills and missing paychecks, you might be owed a lot more than you think.
3. The adjuster who sounds so friendly on the phone? Their job is to pay you as little as possible. They get bonuses for it.
4. Insurance companies posted $95 billion in profits in the first half of 2024. That money came from somewhere. It came from people like you accepting less than they deserved.

7. Still in pain weeks after your accident? You're not imagining it. 50% of car accident victims experience delayed symptoms that get worse, not better.

### Empowerment / David vs. Goliath (You Deserve to Fight Back)

1. The insurance company has adjusters, lawyers, and scripts designed to minimize your payout. You deserve someone fighting for you, too.
2. You didn't cause this accident. You shouldn't have to pay for it. A free case review can show you what your claim is actually worth.
3. They have a team of lawyers. Shouldn't you?
4. You're one person going up against a billion-dollar insurance company. That's not a fair fight, and it doesn't have to be.
5. 91% of people who hire a lawyer get a payout. Only 51% of people who go it alone get anything at all.
6. You stood up for your family every day before this accident. Now it's time to stand up for yourself.

### Money / Settlement Value (What Your Case Could Be Worth)

1. People who hire a lawyer after a car accident receive 3.5x more on average than those who don't. Even after attorney fees, they still take home 3x more.
2. The average car accident settlement is $37,000. How much could YOUR case be worth? Find out in under 2 minutes.
3. Were you in a car accident in [State]? You might be owed more than you think. See what your case could be worth, for free.
4. The insurance company offered you thousands. Your case might be worth tens of thousands. A free 60-second review can tell you the difference.

6. Injured in a car accident? Don't guess what your case is worth. Find out for free in 60 seconds.

### Skepticism / Anti-Lawyer Stigma (We're Different)

1. Think all personal injury lawyers are ambulance chasers? Most work on contingency, meaning they only get paid if YOU get paid. Their success depends on yours.
2. "I was nervous to hire a lawyer because I was already overwhelmed by everything involving the accident." That's what most people say, right before they wish they'd called sooner.
3. You don't need a pushy lawyer. You need someone who explains things in plain English, fights for what's fair, and doesn't charge you a dime unless they win.
4. Scared of lawyers? The insurance company is counting on that. They know people without attorneys accept lower settlements.
5. A good lawyer doesn't add stress to your life. They remove it. They handle the insurance company, the paperwork, and the negotiation so you can focus on healing.
6. The insurance company told you that you'll keep more money without a lawyer. That is exactly what they want you to believe, and the data proves them wrong.

### Urgency / Statute of Limitations (Time Is Running Out)

1. In most states, you have just 2-3 years to file a personal injury claim. Some states give you only 1 year. The clock started the day of your accident.
2. Every week you wait, evidence disappears, witnesses forget, and the insurance company builds its case against you.
3. The statute of limitations does not pause because you're still in pain. If you were hurt in a car accident, find out your deadline before it passes.
4. The insurance company is in no hurry. They know that the longer they delay, the more desperate you become. Don't let them run out your clock.
5. Waiting won't make the bills go away. It won't make the pain stop. And it definitely won't make your claim stronger. A free case review takes 60 seconds.
6. Most people who miss their filing deadline didn't even know there was one. Don't let that be you.

### Objection Handle / "I Was Partially At Fault" (You May Still Qualify)

1. Think you can't file a claim because the accident was partially your fault? In most states, you can still recover compensation, even if you were 30% or 40% at fault.
2. Were you a few miles over the speed limit? Changed lanes right before impact? That does NOT automatically disqualify you. Most states reduce your payout by your percentage of fault instead of eliminating it entirely.
3. The insurance company told you the accident was your fault. That does not make it true, and it does not mean your case is worthless.
4. "I thought because I was partially at fault, I had no case." Millions of Americans recover compensation every year in shared-fault accidents. See if you qualify.
5. Only 4 states completely bar you from recovery if you share any fault. If you don't live in one of those states, you may still have a valid claim worth thousands.
6. The insurance adjuster said you had "the last clear chance to avoid the collision." That's a trained tactic designed to make you accept blame. Get a free review before you accept their version of events.

### Social Proof / Relatability (Other Accident Victims' Experiences)

1. "They got me significantly more than I expected and more than they initially expected. They really do fight for you." See if you have a case, for free.
2. "It's the best decision I ever made. They answered every call rapidly and with respect." Most people who hire a PI lawyer say the same thing: I wish I'd done it sooner.
