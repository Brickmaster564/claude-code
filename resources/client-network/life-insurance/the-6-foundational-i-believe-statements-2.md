🎯 The 6 Foundational “I Believe” Statements
	•	“I believe that life insurance is a way to protect my loved ones from financial stress, not just something for wealthy or older people.” Shifts belief from “I don’t need this yet” to “This is about being a responsible provider now.”
	•	“I believe that getting life insurance is simple, fast, and something I can do entirely online without talking to pushy agents.” Neutralizes friction from fear of complex processes, paperwork, and sales pressure.
	•	“I believe that life insurance is affordable—even a basic plan gives real protection and peace of mind.” Tackles the myth that all coverage is expensive or out of reach financially.
	•	“I believe that it’s better to get some coverage now than wait for the ‘perfect time’ and risk leaving my family unprotected.” Counteracts procrastination rooted in discomfort or perfectionism.
	•	“I believe that I deserve to feel secure knowing my family will be okay even if something unexpected happens to me.” Builds emotional permission and self-worth around taking action.
	•	“I believe that not having coverage is a bigger risk than getting started today with a free quote.” Turns inaction into the real liability—driving urgency without fear-mongering.
