# THRST Winning Scripts

Scripts that have been workshopped and approved for Mike Thurston's quiz funnel ads. Use these as the quality benchmark when writing new scripts.

## Quality DNA

Every script here shares these traits:
- Dead simple language. No marketing speak, no jargon.
- Sounds like Mike talking to camera, not a copywriter writing an ad.
- Leads with a pain or frustration the audience already feels.
- Positions the fix as simple: match training to your body and lifestyle.
- Stacks Mike's credibility naturally within the flow, never as a separate "about me" section.
- CTA is low friction: free quiz, free plan, just click Learn More.
- Every word fights for its place. If it doesn't earn the spot, it gets cut.

## Anti-Patterns

- "Moving the needle", "unlock your potential", or any phrase that sounds like a marketing blog.
- Selling the quiz at the top of the script. Build the open loop first, pitch the solution at the bottom.
- Bridges like "That's why I built THRST." The flow should be seamless, one thought rolling into the next.
- Staccato fragments in sequence ("Simple. Clean. Done.").
- "It's not X, it's not Y" openers (overused in fitness ads).
- Third person ("most people") when you should be calling the audience out directly ("you").
