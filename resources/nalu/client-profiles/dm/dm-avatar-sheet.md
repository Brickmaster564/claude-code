# Client Avatar Sheet

## Demographics

-   **Age Range:** 24--35\
-   **Gender:** Predominantly male (85--95%)\
-   **Location:** English-speaking markets (UK, US, Canada, Australia,
    Ireland)\
-   **Monthly Revenue:** £2,000--£8,000 personal income (common range
    £3,000--£5,500)\
-   **Professional Backgrounds:** Sales, finance, tech, trades,
    fitness-adjacent, junior managers, self-employed founders\
-   **Typical Identities:** Gym regular, trying to look athletic, lifts
    3--5x/week, wants visible results, values efficiency, hates feeling
    like a beginner again

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## 🚩 Key Challenges & Pain Points

### 1. Plateau and Slow Visible Results

-   Trains consistently but physique is not changing fast enough\
-   Strength increases stall or feel random\
-   Feels stuck in the same look despite effort

### 2. Confusion and Second-Guessing

-   Unsure if training volume, split, exercise selection, or recovery is
    the real issue\
-   Unsure if they should cut, maintain, or lean bulk\
-   Overwhelmed by conflicting advice online

### 3. Nutrition and Tracking Friction

-   Knows diet matters, struggles to stay consistent\
-   Macro tracking feels tedious and mentally draining\
-   On track Monday--Thursday, off-plan by the weekend

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## 🌟 Goals & Aspirations

### Short-Term Goals

-   Drop body fat without losing strength\
-   Break the plateau and see measurable progress in 4--8 weeks\
-   Get a clearer, more defined physique (upper abs, shoulders, arms)

### Long-Term Aspirations

-   Maintain an athletic, lean build year-round\
-   Feel confident in fitted clothes and photos\
-   Build identity as disciplined, consistent, the guy who has it
    handled

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## 🧠 Emotional Drivers & Psychological Insights

-   Needs visible feedback to stay motivated\
-   Feels pressure to look the part socially (dating, nights out,
