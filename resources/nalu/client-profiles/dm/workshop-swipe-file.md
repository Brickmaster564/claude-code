> We're going to build your Target Operating Model (TOM) — your business on one page.

>
> Because it shows the flow, it also reveals upstream causes, so you fix the source, not the symptom.

> Bad hires cost you at least £50k each. Worse, they drain your time, kill momentum, and force you back into the weeds.
>
> You're hiring on gut feel, hoping it works out. This system replaces guesswork with process. It makes 85% of your hires the right fit, first time.
>
> We'll build a proper scorecard, turn it into a job ad, and create screening questions that filter out the wrong people before they even hit 'apply'. It adds capacity, not chaos.

## Writing Patterns & Conventions

### How Workshop Copy Differs from Cohort Copy

| | Workshop | Cohort |
|---|---|---|
| **Tone** | More direct, more urgency, more raw | More measured, more selective |
| **CTA** | "Reserve my seat" / "Book your seat now" | "Book a fit call — if it's a fit, I'll invite you in" |
| **Scarcity** | Hard scarcity ("16 spots, once they're gone, they're gone") | Soft scarcity (small group, selective) |
| **Promise framing** | Deliverables-led (you leave with X, Y, Z) | Outcome-led (earn back a day/week, £100k cash-flow) |
| **Agitation depth** | Goes harder on pain — "stressful job", "burn you out", "crap holidays" | More subtle — "decisions and escalations still find you" |
| **Guarantee** | Implicit (deliverables are the guarantee — you walk out with them) | Explicit money-back (90-day outcome guarantee) |
| **Price framing** | Stated upfront, low barrier (£495 + VAT) | Positioned as investment (£2k start + £3k/month) |

### Landing Page Structure (Reusable)
1. **H1 + logistics** (what, when, price — all upfront)
2. **Sub-headline** (single outcome promise)
3. **Deliverables guarantee** (bullet list of what you leave with)
4. **Pain agitation** (three escalating blocks: daily reality > hustle doesn't scale > you're a business risk)
5. **Transition line** ("Let's change that")
6. **Credibility** (first person, personal story, numbers)
7. **CTA button** (mid-page)
8. **What you'll do** (framing line + two systems explained)
9. **Close / final CTA** (urgency + scarcity + date/time/location)
