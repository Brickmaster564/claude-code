## What's Inside

### System 1: Target Operating Model (TOM)
Build the business on one page:
- Map how value flows from lead to cash
- Put one clear owner in each box (no committees)
- RAG it together (red/amber/green) so it's obvious what's working and where the constraints are
- Reveals upstream causes — fix the source, not the symptom

### System 2: A-Player Hiring System
Replace guesswork with process (85% right-fit hires, first time):
- Build a proper scorecard
- Turn it into a job ad
- Create screening questions that filter out wrong people before they hit "apply"
- Adds capacity, not chaos

### What You Leave With
- A completed 90-day implementation plan with biggest constraint identified
- The decision ownership framework (digital + physical workbook)
- The A-player hiring system with scorecards and interview templates
- Access to custom hiring GPTs
- Everything needed to start winning back time the day after the workshop
