## Company – Monkhouse & Company

**Positioning:** Scale-up coaching for founder-CEOs who are stuck in "No Man's Land" – high growth, rising complexity, leadership bottlenecks.

### Offerings

**Premium Leadership Team Program**
- CEO + leadership team do a two-day kickoff at the Farm, quarterly off-sites, and coaching between sessions.
- Format: In-person off-sites + ongoing coaching
- Ideal fit: Scale-ups ~50-100+ headcount needing operating system and leadership cadence

**1:1 CEO Coaching**
- Direct coaching for founder-CEOs of smaller or venture-backed companies.
- Time-limited – capacity constraints led to cohort development.

**Cohort Program (New – Pre-launch)**
- Group for founder-CEOs to learn frameworks, reclaim time, and scale with peer support.
- Goal: Move CEO from firefighting to strategic focus using core playbooks.

### Frameworks

**Seven-Stage Founder's Curve**
- Purpose: Diagnose stage, especially "Frustration" and "No Man's Land" inflection.
- Stages: Startup → Investment → Lift Off → Validation → Frustration → No Man's Land → Future (growth/decline)

**Two-Day-a-Week CEO Blueprint**
- Purpose: Reduce operational drag; shift CEO to high-ROI strategic work.
- Outcome: From 7 days firefighting to 2 days running ops, 3 days on uniquely-CEO work.

## ICP

**Who:** Founder-CEOs of scale-ups
**Region Priority:** UK (London/South East focus); international clients welcome if willing to travel to the Farm

### Firmographics

| Field | Detail |
|---|---|
| **Headcount** | 50-100+ employees (pain often intensifies at 70-100) |
| **Revenue** | £10M-£200M+ |
| **Model** | Bootstrapped or VC-backed |
| **Stage** | Post-PMF, rapid growth, hitting organisational complexity |

### Pain Points

- CEO burnout: 7 days/week, 14-hour days, constant firefighting
- Leadership gaps: weak or missing exec team; hiring/onboarding not systematised
- What worked before now stalls; strategy execution slows
- Operational chaos: crisis management in short bursts; margins/quality at risk

### Psychographics

- Hands-on, scrappy builders struggling to let go
- Outcome-driven, value clear frameworks and peer learning
- Ready to invest for time back, clarity, and scalable systems

### Buying Triggers

- Crossing ~50-100 headcount
- Growth plateaus or wheels starting to come off
- Major life pressure (family/time), risk of burnout/divorce
- Need to prepare for funding, new markets, or exit

### Negative Signals

- Unwillingness to delegate or adopt cadence/operating system
- Seeking vanity metrics over operational change
