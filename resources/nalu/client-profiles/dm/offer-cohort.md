# Offer #1: CEO² Growth Cohort

## What It Is

A quarterly, in-person one-day intensive for a maximum of 10 founder-CEOs. Led by Dominic Monkhouse. Designed to take the weight of daily operations off the founder's shoulders and put the leadership team in total control.

- **Format:** One full day, in-person (9am–5pm, drinks and chat afterwards)
- **Group size:** Maximum 10 founder-CEOs
- **Frequency:** Every quarter
- **Next session:** 21st April
- **Location:** Foundry Farm, Kiln Lane, Redlynch, SP5 2HT
- **Parking:** On-site
- **Public transport:** Trains to Salisbury from London Waterloo; cab rank at station for the last leg

---

## Who It's For

Founder-CEOs running businesses between £2m–£30m with teams of 50–100 people who are still the operational bottleneck.

**Apply if you are:**
- A founder-CEO turning over £3–10 million
- Working too many hours but ready to change
- Serious about building systems and leaders that scale
- Hungry to make the business great and a great place to work

**Not for you if you are:**
- Still chasing product-market fit
- Looking for a course rather than transformation
- Happy being the hero who fixes everything
- Unwilling to be challenged

---

## Core Promise

Get three days back every week. Stop being the bottleneck. Build a leadership team that owns outcomes. Install an operating cadence so the business scales past 100 people without the founder in the middle of everything.

### Three Outcome Pillars

1. **Get the founder out of the bottleneck** — Free up time to do the work only founder-CEOs can do
2. **Build a leadership team that owns outcomes** — So the weight isn't all on the founder
3. **Install an operating cadence** — A repeatable rhythm so the business runs without the founder holding it together

---

## What's Inside

The core programme includes:

- **Hiring Clinic** — Fix the hiring system so the right people are in the right seats
- **Cash Flow Story** — Unlock hidden cash-flow improvements (~£100k target within 90 days)
- **Target Operating Model (TOM)** — Define what the founder should own, what the team should own, and the weekly rhythm that keeps it that way

Plus:
- Peer pressure that helps — work alongside founder-CEOs who keep you honest and share what works
- Led by someone who's done it — Dominic built and sold two £30m companies, coached 100+ founders, a dozen exits

---

## Key Messaging Angles

| Angle | Core Idea |
|---|---|
| **Bottleneck** | You are the reason your business can't grow and you don't even know it |
| **Exhaustion** | Revenue is up, team is bigger, and you're more tired than three years ago |
| **Hiring paradox** | You've hired smart people but the business still depends on you |

| **Role hasn't scaled** | The business has scaled but your role inside it hasn't |
| **Dependency** | Decision rights and standards still live in your head |

## Positioning Summary

This isn't a course. It's a one-day transformation session with ongoing support. Intimate (max 10), in-person (not Zoom), led by an operator who's lived it (not just a coach), with a money-back guarantee tied to measurable outcomes. The CTA is a fit call — Dominic invites you in if it's a fit.
