# Winning Ad Scripts — Client Acquisition (Life Insurance Agencies)

These are proven scripts for acquiring life insurance agency clients for Client Network's PPL lead gen service. The avatar is an AI UGC character speaking as an insurance professional endorsing the lead partner.

## Quality DNA

- Lead with results, not the pitch
- Concrete numbers (lead volume, cost per policy, timeframes)
- Speak as an insider, not a salesman
- Open loops early to retain attention
- Natural CTA that feels like a recommendation, not a hard sell
- 30-40 seconds max. Every sentence earns its place.
