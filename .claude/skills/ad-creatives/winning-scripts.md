# Winning Ad Scripts Reference

These are proven, high-performing ad scripts that set the quality benchmark for all video script rehash work. The DNA in these scripts applies universally across verticals, not just life insurance.

## Quality DNA

Every script here shares these traits:
- Short, punchy sentences. No filler or waffle.
- Lead with the emotional reality the ICP already feels (guilt, anxiety, "what if").
- Reframe the product as relief, not a sale.
- Concrete numbers (coverage amounts, daily cost comparisons).
- Simple CTA: free tool, few questions, no obligation.
- Story-driven or direct address. Never corporate or educational tone.
- Mass appeal, high ceiling: universally relatable to anyone in the ICP.
- Accessible language. No jargon. Reads like a real person talking.

## Anti-Patterns (What Bad Looks Like)

- Too much emotional wandering before getting to the point
- Corporate/educational tone ("Studies show that...")
- Jargon or industry language the ICP doesn't use
- Weak, vague CTAs
- Copy that sounds like a pitch instead of a conversation
- Narrow appeal (only resonates with a small segment of the ICP)
