STW guests must be active UK founder-CEOs of companies with real, proven scale AND a compelling personal narrative. Running a profitable B2B is not enough. They need to be interesting.

**Why:** Two rounds on 2026-03-14. R1: 13/34 kept (38%). R2: 8/30 kept (27%). Main R2 failures: low LinkedIn followers (sub-5K), boring/generic B2B profiles (Nnamdi Emelifeonwu, Stephen Critchlow, Gerald Chappell), fashion brands (George Heaton), founder not physically UK-based (Erkin Adylov in New York), companies not recognizable enough to drive listens.

**How to apply:**
- Before including any STW candidate, verify: (1) active founder/CEO physically based in UK, (2) 30+ employees, (3) active LinkedIn with 5K+ followers and recent posts, (4) company is recognizable OR founder has a compelling story, (5) bookable, (6) passes the "would I click play?" test.
- Sweet spot: founders of companies valued 50M-2B with strong LinkedIn presence. Think Gousto, Cleo, Huma, Dexory, Pip & Nut.
- Automatic cuts: sub-5K LinkedIn followers, no LinkedIn posts, fashion/streetwear/beauty, not physically UK-based, boring B2B without narrative hook, too famous, too small, dated exit.
